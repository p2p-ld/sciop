About SciOp

SciOp is made by a group of researchers, archivists, and hackers — all united in being information activists.

SciOp is the curated catalog/index of Safeguarding Research & Culture (SRC), an alternative infrastructure for archiving and disseminating of cultural heritage and scientific knowledge.
We seek to preserve cultural memory in a way that traditional archives cannot:

Together, we can ensure that our cultural, intellectual and scientific heritage exists in multiple copies, in multiple places, and that no single entity or group of entities can make it all disappear.
Because people (and our diversity) matter.
